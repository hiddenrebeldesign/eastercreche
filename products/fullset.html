﻿ Full Easter Crèche set reproduced from original sculptures by artist Chris Smith. The set includes figure of the Resurrected Christ, the Garden Tomb and movable stone, the mother Mary, the Angel, 2 Roman Guards, 3 Palm Trees, Flowers and Pots. The tomb is a hollow-cavity, artistic reproduction inspired by the actual garden tomb in Jerusalem. Its size is designed to allow the 5” Christ figurine to be placed inside the tomb. The separate freestanding stone can be moved to cover the tomb entrance for a fun and interactive experience as you use your Easter Crèche to re-enact and teach your family the Easter story.
